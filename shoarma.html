Description

Grilled meat in a wrap with a sweet and spicy sauce.

really easy to make and good to eat anytime of the day.

Instructions

  1. Get some kind of meat to your liking and cut it into small pieces (~1/2 inch)

  2. Let's prepare the seasoning for the meat

    • 2 tablespoons of coriander
    • 2 tablespoons of cumin
    • 1.5 tablespoons of paprika
    • 1 tablespoon of turmeric
    • 1 tablespoon of onion powder
    • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
    • 1 tablespoon of cinnamon
    • 1 tablespoon of black pepper
    • 1 tablespoon of salt
    • 1/2 tablespoon of parsley
    • 1 teaspoon of pepper flakes
  3. Heat a pan on medium/high heat and add some oil.
  4. Add the meat to the pan and add some of the seasoning.
  5. while the meat is cooking we can make our sauce. Add the following ingredients together in a small bowl.
    • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise.
    • 1 tablespoon of honey.
    • 1 tablespoon of hot sauce of your liking.
    • (optional) teaspoon of parsley
  6. once the meat is fully cooked, add some meat and sauce on a wrap and enjoy!
