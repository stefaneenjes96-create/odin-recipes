Baked Chicken

Home

Description

Baked chicken with a sweet and spicy rub.

Why buy a rotisserie chicken from the store when you can make a better one at home.

Instructions

  1. Let's start with making the seasoning. Add the following ingredients in a small bowl

    • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
    • 1 1/3 tablespoon of thyme
    • 2 teaspoons of salt
    • 2 teaspoons of paprika
    • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
    • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  2. Rub the seasoning all over the chicken. Don't forget to put some under the skin.
  3. Pre-heat oven to 400 F and put in the chicken for about an hour or until internal temp reached 165 F
  4. let the chicken rest for 5 minutes and enjoy!
